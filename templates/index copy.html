About me

I’m Sofifi, an illustrator from Moscow. Through my work, I explore emotion and storytelling — creating quiet, magical worlds where reality blends with fantasy. My art speaks through light, color, and texture, giving shape to feelings like wonder, fear, hope, and gentle sadness. I draw inspiration from nature, music, and my inner world. While I work digitally (mostly in Procreate), I aim for a soft, hand-painted feel. I love telling stories in a single frame — with expressive characters, rich details, and a sense of mystery. I regularly take part in contests and open calls, always looking to grow and connect. For me, art is a doorway — a quiet place where someone might see a piece of their own story.
